Mr. Venkatesh Prabhu, Sc ’F’

Mr. Venkatesh Prabhu received his BE degree in Electrical & Electronics from Regional Engineering College, Suratkal, in 1999 and his ME degree from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, in 2001.

Since 2001, he has been with the Electronics & Radar Development Establishment (LRDE), Bangalore, where he has been involved in the design and development of high-power radar transmitters and antenna array power supplies. He has contributed to the indigenous development and realization of high-power radar transmitters for 3D Surveillance Radars, Weapon Locating Radar, and Synthetic Aperture Radars.
